About me

My name is Jack Blake and I am a strategic analytics consultant at IBM. I work within the Business Dynamics team: a part of IBM's Global Business Services that focuses on using data and advanced analytics to solve clients' problems.

Before this I completed a PhD in Mathematics at the University of Bath. My PhD focused on neutron transport and reactor criticality within the field of numerical analysis, and was sponsored by AMEC Foster Wheeler and EPSRC via an industrial CASE award. A copy of my thesis can be downloaded here.
