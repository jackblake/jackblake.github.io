About Me

My name is Jack Blake, and I am a fourth year PhD research student studying mathematics at the University of Bath. I work with Ivan Graham, Alastair Spence and Paul Smith, who are supervising my research. My PhD is focused on neutron transport and reactor criticality, and I am working in the field of numerical analysis. My work is generously sponsored by AMEC Foster Wheeler and EPSRC.
